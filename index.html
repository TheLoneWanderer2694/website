📲 How to Cast from Phone (Working Method)

1 Same WiFi
Connect phone & Chromecast to same network
2 Start Video
Select a video above or upload from your phone
3 Tap Cast Icon
Look for the cast icon in the video player controls (bottom right of video)
Chrome automatically shows this on mobile when Chromecast is detected
4 Select Device
Choose your Chromecast from the popup

⚠️ Important

The Chromecast SDK does not work in mobile browsers. Use Chrome's built-in video casting instead:

• Look for the cast icon inside the video player itself
• NOT a separate button on the page
• Appears automatically when Chromecast is on your network
