📲 How to cast from your phone:

  1. Same WiFi: Connect phone & Chromecast to same network
  2. Select video: Tap "select video from phone" above OR enter URL
  3. Tap cast icon: Choose your Chromecast device
  4. Control: Use this screen as remote (play, pause, seek)
